Our Story, Passion & Commitment To Beauty

For years, Yoly Hair Salon has helped women look and feel beautiful through professional styling, braiding, coloring, treatments and beauty care services.

Who We Are

Yoly Hair Salon is more than a beauty salon. We are a destination for confidence, elegance, self-expression and transformation.

Located in Orange Farm, Johannesburg, we proudly provide professional hair care services to clients seeking quality, creativity and exceptional customer service.

Every hairstyle is created with attention to detail, ensuring that every client leaves feeling empowered and beautiful.
